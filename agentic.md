## Agentic AI & Tools Summary 2026

### Key Findings from Web Search:

1. **Agentic AI Evaluation Beyond Output Quality (2026)**
   - Teams can no longer evaluate agentic AI only by whether the final output looks useful.
   - Production agents need to be measured on: task success, tool correctness, latency, retries, policy compliance, and escalation handling.

2. **Coding Agents Reshaping Development**
   - The distinction between "people who code" and "people who don't" is permeable in 2026.
   - Agentic workflows are integrated into platforms like Legora (AI-powered legal platform).
   - This suggests agents can handle more complex, domain-specific tasks beyond simple coding.

### Implications:
- The agentic model requires robust monitoring systems to track agent performance metrics.
- Tools should be designed with proper escalation paths and error handling.
- There's significant trust in the automation of knowledge-intensive workflows.

This summary is based on web search results from 2026 and reflects the current state of agentic AI development as understood through available sources. The information may evolve beyond this date.
